About FOLO

Folo is a wanderer trapped in the mysterious world of Ruins. His ultimate goal is to escape this forgotten realm and reach the legendary White Dimension.

Along his journey, he faces countless adventures and encounters strange and fascinating creatures that inhabit the Ruins. Each new discovery helps Folo uncover the secrets of this world, and every meeting teaches him something new about its nature.

Folo records all of his findings in his personal journal — a growing chronicle of his path, thoughts, and discoveries. The beings he encounters are brought to life not only on the pages of his book but also as a unique NFT collection, allowing anyone to become part of his unfolding story.
